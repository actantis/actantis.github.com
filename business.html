Business Manifesto

Vision

Unified Transportation Platform for Dynamic Personal Travel Routing and Predictive Transport Data Analytics

Mission

External – Connectivity and Convergence for Better Public Transportation and Intelligent Mobility Internal – Eliminate Waste and Amplify Learning for Agile Business and Lean-thinking Enterprise

Motto

External – Towards Wealth through Science (“Ad Lucrum per Scientia”) Internal – Doing the Right Things in the Right Way at the Right Time

Aspirations

Disrupt Smart Transportation Market and Build New Generation Systems for Intelligent Mobility

Aims

Liberate – passengers travel routing for intelligent mobility Improve – public transportation services and predictability Globalise – optimisation services and predictive analytics

Values

Reasonable Simplicity – easy to understand, explain and implement solutions, products, services, systems Pragmatic Creativity – degree of autonomy and openness to add value to products, services and systems Purposeful Improvement – on-going effort to continuously improve team, products, services and systems Personal Integrity – honesty and consistency of values, goals, principles, actions, expectations and attitude

Objectives

Clarity – build simple, agile and scalable business system Quality – deliver outstanding solutions, products and services Efficiency – optimise income, operation cost and working capital

Practices

Lean Entrepreneurship – actions from one centrepoint Leadership Culture – breathing into supportive centre Thinking Environment – practitioner reflection method

Activities

Business Development – build sustainable business model servicing target markets Product Development – design product offerings addressing real problems and needs Systems Development – develop operations and systems supporting business goals

Approach

Discover – investigate subjects to gain insight or knowledge of it for future development stages Define – plan approach, timelines and budget to support the continuous integration and delivery Decide – agree actions plan to create a baseline for the measurement of the continuous delivery Design – construct a future state of solutions, products, services and systems for development Develop – build modules of solutions, products, services and systems for continuous deployment Describe – prepare handover for continuous testing to meet user, customer or client requirements Detect – test production modules or software builds against the criteria (eg quality, security, stability) Deploy – rollout production modules or software builds to prepare for the release into production Deliver – release production modules or software builds to live environment for customers or clients Discuss – post implementation review to purposefully improve the continuous integration and delivery

Measurement

On-time – delivery within objective timelines and available capacity to keep dependency to the minimum Budget – delivery within allocated resources to keep the operational cost and CapEx at manageable levels Quality – delivery within shared perceptions and known expectations increasing value and reducing waste
