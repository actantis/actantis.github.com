    Business Manifesto

    Vision

    Unified Transportation Platform for Dynamic Personal Travel Routing and Predictive Transport Data Analytics

    Mission

    External – Connectivity and Convergence for Better Public Transportation and Intelligent Mobility
    Internal – Eliminate Waste and Amplify Learning for Agile Business and Lean-thinking Enterprise

    Motto

    External – Towards Wealth through Science (“Ad Lucrum per Scientia”)
    Internal – Doing the Right Things in the Right Way at the Right Time

    Aspirations

    Disrupt Smart Transportation Market and Build New Generation Systems for Intelligent Mobility

    Aims

    01. Liberate – passengers travel routing for intelligent mobility
    02. Improve – public transportation services and predictability
    03. Globalise – optimisation services and predictive analytics

    Values

    01. Reasonable Simplicity – easy to understand, explain, implement solutions, products, services, systems
    02. Pragmatic Creativity – degree of autonomy and openness to add value to products, services, systems
    03. Purposeful Improvement – on-going effort to continuously improve team, products, services, systems
    04. Personal Integrity – honesty and consistency of values, goals, principles, actions, expectations, attitude

    Objectives

    01. Clarity – build simple, agile and scalable business system
    02. Quality – deliver outstanding solutions, products and services
    03. Efficiency – optimise income, operation cost and working capital

    Practices

    01. Lean Entrepreneurship – actions from one centrepoint
    02. Leadership Culture – breathing into supportive centre
    03. Thinking Environment – practitioner reflection method

    Activities

    01. Business Development – build sustainable business model servicing target markets
    02. Product Development – design product offerings addressing real problems and needs
    03. Systems Development – develop operations and systems supporting business goals

    Approach

    01. Discover – investigate subjects to gain insight or knowledge of it for future development stages
    02. Define – plan approach, timelines and budget to support the continuous integration and delivery
    03. Decide – agree actions plan to create a baseline for the measurement of the continuous delivery
    04. Design – construct a future state of solutions, products, services and systems for development
    05. Develop – build modules of solutions, products, services and systems for continuous deployment
    06. Describe – prepare handover for continuous testing to meet user, customer or client requirements
    07. Detect – test production modules or software builds against criteria (quality, security, stability)
    08. Deploy – rollout production modules or software builds to prepare for the release into production
    09. Deliver – release production modules or software builds to live environment for customers or clients
    10. Discuss – post implementation review to purposefully improve the continuous integration and delivery

    Measurement

    01. On-time – delivery within objective timelines & available capacity to keep dependency to the minimum
    02. Budget – delivery within allocated resources to keep the operational cost & CapEx at manageable levels
    03. Quality – delivery within shared perceptions and known expectations increasing value & reducing waste
